tidyr 0.3.0

2015-09-13 Hadley Wickham
tidyr 0.3.0 is now available on CRAN. tidyr makes it easy to “tidy” your data, storing it in a consistent form so that it’s easy to manipulate, visualise and model. Tidy data has variables in columns and observations in rows, and is described in more detail in the tidy data vignette. Install tidyr with: install.packages("tidyr") tidyr contains four new verbs: fill(), replace() and complete(), and unnest(), and lots of smaller bug fixes and improvements. Read more →
